    • Extensive and Intensive Properties
    Lesson 4
    Extensive property - An extensive property is a property that depends on the amount of matter.
    Ex: Volume, mass, volume, size, weight and length.

    Intensive property - Intensive properties do not depend on the quantity of matter. Intensive properties serve to characterise matter.
    Ex: Color, density, boiling point and melting point
