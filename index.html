    • Welcome to Quiznism!

    An online quiz reviewer to help you improve your knowledge in Chemistry.

    Rules
    1. You can't exit once you continue.
    2. Once you select a lesson, you can't go back till you finish the quiz.
    3. You will have only 30 seconds per each question.
    4. Once you select your answer, it can't be undone.
    5. You can't select any option once time goes off.
    6. You will have choose a lesson to take the quiz.
    7. You'll get points on the basis of your correct answers.
    8. You can review the lesson after you've completed the exam.
    9. You can retake the quiz anytime you want.
